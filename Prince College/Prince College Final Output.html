Admission Process

1

Check Eligibility

10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, Math (PCM) with min. 45% aggregate. Tamil Nadu State Board / CBSE / ISC accepted.

2

Register on TNEA Portal

Apply via Tamil Nadu Engineering Admissions (TNEA) portal or direct Management Quota application.

3

Document Verification

Submit academic certificates, community certificate, Transfer Certificate, and Nativity Certificate.

4

Counseling & Allotment

Attend TNEA counseling. Enter Counseling Code 1442 to choose PDKVCET as your preference.

5

Fee Payment & Enrollment

Pay the semester fee and complete enrollment formalities at the college campus.
